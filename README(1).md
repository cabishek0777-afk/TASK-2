# Task 2: Analyze a Phishing Email Sample

## Objective
Identify phishing characteristics in a suspicious email sample as part of the Cyber Security Internship (Elevate Labs).

## What I Did
1. Obtained a sample phishing email impersonating PayPal.
2. Examined the sender's email address and display name for spoofing (`PayPaI` / `paypa1-support.com`).
3. Ran the email headers through an online header analyzer to check SPF, DKIM, and DMARC results.
4. Identified the mismatched hyperlink (displayed PayPal URL vs. actual malicious `.ru` link).
5. Noted urgency/threatening language used to pressure the recipient.
6. Checked for spelling and grammar errors in the email body.
7. Compiled all findings into a phishing indicators report.

## Files in This Repo
- `Phishing_Email_Analysis_Report.md` — Full report with the sample email, header analysis table, and list of phishing indicators found.
- `screenshots/` — Screenshot(s) of the header analyzer output (add your own).

## Tools Used
- Sample phishing email (text)
- Free online header analyzer (e.g., MxToolbox, Google Admin Toolbox Messageheader)

## Outcome
Gained practical awareness of phishing tactics, email spoofing techniques, and how to analyze email headers to detect threats.

## Key Concepts
Phishing, Email Spoofing, Header Analysis, Social Engineering, Threat Detection
